What is concurrent H1B employment?

As per USCIS Glossary

Approved H-1B petitions with “New concurrent employment” selected on Part 2, Question 2 of the Form I-129. The beneficiary will begin new employment with an additional employer in the same nonimmigrant classification the beneficiary currently holds while continuing to work for his or her current employer in the same classification.

In short, the worker already holds H1B status with one employer and is adding a second H1B employer without leaving the classification.

USCIS Form I-129 Part 2

Current H-1B regulations permit individuals to work for multiple employers simultaneously, provided that each employer files a separate petition and the candidate continues to meet all eligibility criteria.

Part 2 — Information About This Petition
